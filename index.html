About NovaCore Hub

Built for the Future of Africa — Starting in Rwanda

NovaCore Hub is where students gain digital experience and businesses get real results. We connect the young generation to modern work environments through internships, tech training, and soft skills. At the same time, we deliver affordable and high-impact digital services to businesses that want to grow, close more sales, and stay competitive.

  • ✓ Hands-on student training programs
  • ✓ Real-world business service delivery
  • ✓ Focused on ROI and client satisfaction
  • ✓ Rwanda-first, Africa-next vision
